Why Ambleside Primary School?

More Than a Good
Education.

At Ambleside Primary School, we believe that the years from Grade 1 to Grade 7 are not just preparation for high school — they are significant years in their own right, deserving of a rich and unhurried education.

Our Charlotte Mason approach means that children encounter the best ideas humanity has produced — through living books, direct observation of the natural world, music and art, and a curriculum that is deliberately broad. We pair this with Singapore Mathematics, which builds deep conceptual understanding rather than superficial procedural skill.

Small class sizes mean our teachers genuinely know every child. That knowledge changes everything about how learning happens.
