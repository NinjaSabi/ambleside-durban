Why Ambleside Primary School?

More Than a Good
Education.

At Ambleside Primary School, we believe that Grades 1 to 7 are not merely preparation for high school — they are significant years in their own right, worthy of a rich and unhurried education.

Our approach means that children encounter the best ideas through living books, direct observation of the natural world, music, art, and a curriculum that is deliberately broad. We pair this with Singapore Math, which builds deep conceptual understanding rather than superficial procedural skill.

Small class sizes mean our teachers genuinely know every child. That knowledge changes everything about how learning happens.

We are mindful of not over-committing children to too many activities. Our sport and extracurricular programme is intentionally balanced to protect family time and the slower pace of learning we believe in.
