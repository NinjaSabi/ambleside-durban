Bursary Fund

Support Ambleside

The Bursary Fund enables families who share our values to give their children an Ambleside education, regardless of financial constraints.

Why We Give

A school for every
family we can reach

Ambleside is a small, affordable school by conviction — not by accident. We believe the kind of education we offer should not be available only to those who can easily afford it. The Bursary Fund is how we live that conviction out.

Every donation goes directly toward supporting families who cannot meet full fees. If you have been shaped by what this school is doing, or if you simply believe in it, we would be honoured by your support.
