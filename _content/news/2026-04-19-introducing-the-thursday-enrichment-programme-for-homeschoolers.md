One of the questions we hear most often from homeschooling families is this: "Where can my child experience structured, enriching learning alongside other children — without having to commit to full-time school?"

We have been thinking about that question for a while. And our answer is the Thursday Enrichment Programme.

Every Thursday during term, we open our school to homeschooled children in Grades 1 to 7 from 10:00 to 12:30. The morning follows a simple, unhurried rhythm: Nature Study outdoors, Composer Study and Music Appreciation, and a shared break for free play.

All materials are provided. Bring a light lunch, a water bottle, and a cap for outside. The cost is R100 for your first child and R80 for each additional child. Booking is essential as spaces are limited.

This programme sits at the heart of what we believe — that learning is richer in community, and that the best things in education are hard to do alone. We are not trying to replace what you have built at home. We are offering something to add to it.

If you would like to book a Thursday, visit our Thursday Enrichment page or email us at [admin@amblesidedurban.com](mailto:admin@amblesidedurban.com).
