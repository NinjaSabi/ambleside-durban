Preschool — Acorns

Preschool
FAQs

The questions preschool families ask most — answered honestly.

What ages does Acorns accept?+

Acorns accepts children from Grade 000 (typically age 3) through Grade R (typically age 5–6). Children move naturally into our Primary School (Grade 1) after completing Grade R.

Is swimming included in the fees?+

Yes — swimming is included in the Acorns programme at no additional cost. All children participate, and they make remarkable progress in a short time.

What are the preschool hours?+

Preschool hours are Monday to Friday, 07:30 to 13:30. Aftercare is available from 13:30 to 17:30 at R1,900 per term or R70 per day.

Will my child transition smoothly into Primary School?+

This is one of the greatest advantages of the Ambleside Acorns programme. Because both schools share the same Charlotte Mason philosophy and teaching approach, the transition from Grade R to Grade 1 is natural and smooth — no culture shock, no adjustment period, just continuation of the same warm learning community.

How do I apply for Acorns?+

Start by booking a school visit. We love meeting prospective families in person. After your visit, we'll walk you through our simple four-step application process. See How to Apply for full details.
