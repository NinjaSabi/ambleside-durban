Admissions

How to
Apply

From first question to first day — a clear, unhurried process.

0
Not Ready Yet?

Send an Enquiry or Take a Virtual Tour

Not sure if Ambleside is right for your family? That is completely fine — and we would love to help you find out. You can send us a question using the form at the bottom of this page, or take a virtual tour via our YouTube channel to get a feel for who we are before committing to a visit. There is no obligation, and no question is too small.

Take a Virtual Tour
1
First Step

Book a School Visit

The best way to know whether Ambleside is right for your family is to come and experience it. We warmly invite all prospective families to visit — meet our teachers, see our classrooms, ask your questions, and get a feel for the Ambleside community. We schedule visits on school days so you can see the school in action. There is no obligation whatsoever.

Book your visit here →

2
Second Step

Assessment — Three Days at Ambleside

Once you are ready to proceed, your child will spend three days with us. This is not a pass/fail test — it is an opportunity for your child to experience Ambleside life, and for us to understand who they are and how we can best serve them. The assessment fee is R850, credited towards your enrolment deposit if you proceed.

Alongside the assessment, you will complete an application form. We ask about your family, your child, and your hopes for their education. This conversation matters to us.

3
Third Step

Feedback Meeting & Offer

After the assessment, we meet with you to share our feedback — what we observed, where your child is, and how we believe Ambleside can support them. If we are a mutual fit, we will extend a formal offer of placement. You will have time to consider it carefully before accepting.

We are honest if we feel the school is not the right fit, and we ask that families are equally honest with us. A good match serves everyone.

4
Final Step

Confirm Enrolment

To confirm your child’s place, a refundable enrolment deposit of R4,500 is required. Stationery is billed separately at R2,500 per year. Once confirmed, you will receive a welcome pack with everything you need to prepare for your child’s first day. We look forward to welcoming your family.

Key Costs at a Glance

Assessment feeR850 (credited on enrolment)
Enrolment depositR4,500 (refundable)
