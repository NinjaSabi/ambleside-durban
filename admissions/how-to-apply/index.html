Admissions

How to
Apply

A simple four-step process — from first enquiry to your child's first day.

1
First Step

Book a School Visit

The best way to know whether Ambleside is right for your family is to come and experience it. We warmly invite all prospective families to visit — meet our teachers, see our classrooms, ask your questions, and get a feel for the Ambleside community. There is no obligation whatsoever.

We schedule visits on school days so that you can see the school in action. Please book your visit here.

2
Second Step

Assessment & Application

Once you are ready to proceed, your child will attend a short assessment. This is not a pass/fail test — it helps us understand where your child is, and how we can best support them. The assessment fee is R850, which is credited towards your enrolment deposit if you proceed.

Alongside the assessment, you will complete an application form. We ask about your family, your child, and your hopes for their education. This conversation matters to us.

3
Third Step

Offer & Acceptance

If we are a mutual fit, we will extend a formal offer of placement. You will receive a letter of offer outlining the grade, start date, and any relevant conditions. You will have time to consider the offer carefully before accepting.

We are honest if we feel the school is not the right fit — and we ask that families are equally honest with us. A good match serves everyone.

4
Final Step

Confirm Enrolment

To confirm your child's place, a refundable enrolment deposit of R4,500 is required. This secures your child's place in their class. Stationery is billed separately at R2,500 per year.

Once confirmed, you will receive a welcome pack with everything you need to prepare for your child's first day. We look forward to welcoming your family.
