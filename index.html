A Living Education

Where learning is a feast,
not a formula.

Ambleside School of Durban is a small, affordable, Christ-centred private school that believes the best education we can give is one that sees children as whole persons and gives them the full richness of human knowledge to engage with. We are not trying to produce children who have the right answers. We are trying to form people of deep character, genuine curiosity, and lasting faith.

If you are looking for an alternative to mainstream, CAPS-driven schooling — one that values real books over screens, relationships over results, and character alongside academic excellence — you may have found your community. We are a deliberate, unhurried school: no tablets in the classroom, no teaching to the test, no factory-line education.

We are a small school by conviction. Small classes mean teachers genuinely know every child — what makes them curious, what makes them flourish, what they are ready for next. We also warmly welcome families transitioning from homeschooling who want the structure and community of school without losing the heart of what they love.

Early Childhood Education · Gr 000 — R

Ambleside Preschool
— Acorns

The early years are not a waiting room for real school — they are among the most formative years of a child's entire life. At Acorns, we take these years seriously, laying foundations of curiosity, character, and love of learning that last a lifetime.

  • Free & structured play every single day
  • Living books, stories & rich read-alouds
  • Early maths, Afrikaans & isiZulu
  • Music literacy, art & handwork
  • Swimming included in programme
  • Bible, nature time & motor skills

From Grade 1 to Grade 7, our primary school offers a rigorous, rich, and deeply human education — preparing children not just for high school, but for a full and meaningful life.

Our Convictions

What We Believe

Our values are not aspirations on a wall. They are lived convictions that shape every decision we make — in the classroom, in our community, and in our relationship with families.

01
Children Are Persons

Each child is a whole person — not a vessel to be filled, but an individual of immeasurable value and potential.

02
Education Is a Feast

The best education we can give is one that is broad, rich, and genuinely enjoyable — a feast of living ideas across every discipline.

03
Character Matters Most

We believe character will ultimately have greater and longer-lasting value than credentials. Small classes make genuine formation possible.

04
Families Are Partners

Education is a whole-family journey. We keep our partnership with parents warm, honest, and mutual.

05
A Living Faith

Our Christian ethos is not a bolt-on. It is the framework through which we understand people, the world, and our purpose.
